About OrgPro

What is OrgPro?

OrgPro is an online platform that allows student organizations to showcase organizational information and manage their memberships. OrgPro help student organizations with logistics by providing single platform to communicate with all members, diseminate the information about events to all members and help keep track of all members due in easy and efficient way.

What features are available?

Some feature available to organizations and members through OrgPro include:

  • View Organization's events calendar
  • RSVP for organization events
  • View events they attended in past
  • Check-in at events with QR code
  • View their rank in the orgnizaion
  • View the top ranked members
  • View and pay their dues
  • View their payment history
