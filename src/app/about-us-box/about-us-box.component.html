About ORG PRO

ORGPRO is an online platform that allows student organizations to showcase organizational information and manage their memberships. OrgPro help student organizations with logistics by providing single platform to communicate with all members, diseminate the information about events to all members and help keep track of all members due in easy and efficient way.

What Can I Do With ORG PRO?

No matter what your background or major is, we welcome you to participate in all of our events and activities! To become a member and keep up with upcoming events, you should join our membership portal, follow our social medias, and sign up for our newsletter. Signing up will keep you up to date with everything ACM, and earning points on the portal might even earn you a prize!
